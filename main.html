Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is often called the "City of Light". It is known for its romantic charm, historic landmarks, world-class museums, and delicious cuisine. Millions of visitors come every year to experience its timeless beauty.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – the iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – home to the famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – the world’s most famous shopping street.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – offering breathtaking views of Paris.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – buttery and flaky French pastries.
  • Crêpes – thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Macarons – colorful, delicate almond cookies.
  • Escargot – a unique French delicacy of snails.
  • French Onion Soup – rich, savory, and topped with cheese.
